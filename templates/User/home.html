What do we do?

Our services

Efficiency in Complaint Filing:

Streamline the process of reporting environmental issues with our user-friendly platform, allowing users to submit complaints quickly and efficiently using photos.This can be anonymously as well as per user requirements.

Contribute to a Larger Movement:

Become part of a global movement for positive environmental change, knowing that your contributions, whether big or small, make a significant impact on issues like deforestation, animal cruelty, and waste management.

Educational Resources:

Access educational content and image recognition guides that empower users to capture relevant and impactful photos, enhancing the overall quality and relevance of reported incidents.
