Eco Voice is a website that has features such as filing complaints, donating money, and sharing blogs. Users can file complaints online for any environmental abuse happening in their vicinity or surrounding areas. Users can also donate money to certain organizations that play a major role in environmental activities. Users can also share their experiences and learn from each other in the blogs section.
